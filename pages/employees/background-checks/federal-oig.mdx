# Federal OIG Checks

> ⚠️ **Read-Only Documentation Notice**  
> This page was generated through passive observation of publicly accessible UI elements. No data was injected, submitted, or modified during documentation creation.

## Overview

Automated screening of employees and contractors against the HHS Office of Inspector General List of Excluded Individuals/Entities (LEIE).

## Key Features

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Functionality">
    - Bulk upload staff identifiers
    - Scheduled re-screening (monthly/quarterly)
    - Alert on new exclusions
    - Documentation for audit trails
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="User Roles">
    - HR/Compliance: Full access
    - Management: Read + Alert config
    - Others: No access
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Related Modules">
    State OIG, DPS Checks, Drug Screening
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

## Workflow

<Steps>
  <Step title="Access">
    Employees → Background Checks → Federal OIG tab
  </Step>
  <Step title="Primary Actions">
    Upload CSV of staff IDs; Configure screening frequency; Review match results; Document resolution
  </Step>
  <Step title="Outputs">
    Screening completion report; Exclusion match alert; Compliance attestation document
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Compliance & Security

<Callout type="info">
  OIG screening is required for Medicaid/Medicare providers. All screenings and resolutions must be documented per 42 CFR §1001. Records retained 10 years.
</Callout>

## Troubleshooting

| Issue | Resolution |
|-------|------------|
| Upload fails | Verify CSV format matches template; Check file size &lt;10MB; Ensure no special characters in names |
