## Welcome

This documentation covers the **OfficeRidge Enterprise Healthcare Management Platform** — a comprehensive solution for home healthcare agencies managing member care, employee compliance, operational workflows, and regulatory requirements.

### 🎯 Platform Purpose

- Streamline member admissions, care planning, and discharge workflows
- Maintain employee compliance with background checks, training, and competency assessments
- Automate task management, reporting, and inter-departmental coordination
- Ensure adherence to HIPAA, CMS, state licensing, and emergency preparedness regulations
- Manage financial operations including payroll, claims, vendor contracts, and tax compliance
