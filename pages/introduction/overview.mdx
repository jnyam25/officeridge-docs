## What Is This OfficeRidge?

OfficeRidge is a comprehensive solution designed for home healthcare agencies to manage:

- 👥 Member admissions, care plans, and patient experience workflows
- 👨‍⚕️ Employee onboarding, compliance, training, and time tracking
- 📋 Task management, reporting, and operational oversight
- 🔐 Licensing, corporate governance, and regulatory compliance
- 💰 Financial operations, payroll, and claims processing

## Key Capabilities

| Module | Primary Function |
|--------|-----------------|
| **Members** | Member lifecycle management from intake to discharge |
| **Employees** | Staff directory, compliance checks, training, and attendance |
| **Task Manager** | Centralized task tracking with email-to-task automation |
| **Resources** | Utilities, bulk tools, and reference materials |
| **Reports** | Customizable reporting across all operational domains |
| **Compliance** | HIPAA, CMS, fraud prevention, and incident tracking |
| **Finance** | Vendor management, budgeting, cost centers, and tax preparation |
